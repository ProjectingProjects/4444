In 1935, Deborah Diovana decided to quit the movie business and set out to become whom she always wanted to be: a chef. She created her first pizzeria in Los Angeles and became a properitor of 30 more within the next 35 years of her life. Her pizza reached all over America, and is now considered the Best Pizzeria in America according to Food Network and the International Pizza Commission of Sicily. Deborah's spirit still drives the business today as the Debi's Pizza crew works hard to meet our goals:

Make Quality Food with a Quality Smile!
